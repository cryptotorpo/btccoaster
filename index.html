History of the Bitcoin Roller Coaster Guy

Marcus Conor made Bitcoin Roller Coaster animated gif in early 2013. The inspiration came from all the talk of the price being a roller coaster ride. He posted it publicly in a few places: on his comic site BrainlessTales.com, on the bitcointalk forums, and on reddit. He has since become widely popular in the Bitcoin community but the initial response was not great. On reddit it was downvoted to 0 and that’s where it remains!
